GAMES

Tic-Tac-Toe

Tic-Tac-Toe is a classic two-player game where players take turns marking X or O on a 3x3 grid. The goal is to form a row, column, or diagonal of the same symbol to win.

Rock Paper Scissor

Rock-Paper-Scissors is a simple hand game played between two players. Each player chooses rock, paper, or scissors, and the winner is decided based on the game's rules.
